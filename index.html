About Us

History

Paul Sutton founded Howe Sound Window Cleaning in 2003 as he recognized a need for such services in the Sea to Sky Corridor. Paul's strong work ethic and attention to detail has helped him grow his business to where it is today. Howe Sound Window Cleaning's friendly, professional and personal management style have, over the years helped them gain a wide range of experience in servicing properties ranging from residential and townhouse complexes to commercial properties.

Our Mission

Howe Sound Window Cleaning is committed to customer satisfaction by meeting or exceeding the highest standards of service, and to deliver this service with an open caring and thoughtful management style. As professionals, clients can expect that we will not sublet or subcontract accounts. Our mission of excellence will also include maintaining a full compliment of insurance.
